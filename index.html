Masters Research student at IIIT-H under the guidance of Prof C V Jawahar in CVIT lab. I'm interested in Machine Learning, Statistics and its applications on Computer Vision. I'm currently working on improving the accuracy of facial landmark detection.

Prior joining IIIT-H, I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering at RVCE, Bangalore. Then I worked as ASIC Engineer at Juniper Networks. My work involved modelling certain modules (ex. Arbitration of packets) of networking ASICs for functional verfication.

Apart from the time I spend in the lab, I enjoy going on treks, taking landscape photographs, travelling and cycling. The photo on the homepage is one of my favourite clicks taken during a trek near Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh.
